Item Calibrator see how your test questions performed

Paste your students' scores and find out which questions worked, which were too easy or too hard, and which to fix — plus where each student landed. No sign-up, no upload: everything runs in your browser and your data never leaves this page.

Difficulty
How many students got the question right. A low score means it was hard; a very high one means it may be too easy to be useful.
Discrimination
Whether a question separates students who did well overall from those who didn't. Good questions are answered right more often by stronger students.
Student ability
With Item Response Theory, students and questions are placed on one shared scale, so you can see how able each student is and how well the test targets your class.
